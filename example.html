Sound of Random

Simple library for playing random melodies on pages. It's very short and doesn't require anything.

Homepage: www.sor.doleczek.pl

Try it your self

/

How to use it

Includ library

  <script src="sor.js"></script>

Play sound of random with SoR.play(). To stop simply run SoR.stop().

To check if sound is currantly playing SoR.isPlaying() might be used.
